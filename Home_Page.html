The same waters that soon turn to crash and thrash against the rugged coastline just below – majestic waves surrounding your little sea cottage sanctuary in a swell of white foam. RHYTHM OF NATURE

A holiday at Cliff Cottage, Kilkee, is a holiday tuned to the rejuvenating rhythm of nature. This restored original 1800’s coastal stone cottage is set high on the beautifully breathtaking Cliffs of Kilkee, County Clare – on Ireland’s West Coast.

SECULDED

A secluded safe-haven, the cottage recalls the old days of Irish maritime living – yet is ideally positioned to give you all the attractions and fascination of the Wild Atlantic Way, right on your doorstep.

ELEGANT COMFORT

And because you want to holiday in style, this elegant cottage that sleeps 7 people is perfectly appointed, finished and furnished, offering absolute premium quality and high-end comfort – to see to your every need.
